Guided Hikes

Casita Sedona offers a free guided hike each week for guests. Explore the Seven Sacred Pools along the Soldiers Pass Trail.

Located in the Heart of Sedona

At Casita Sedona Bed & Breakfast you'll be close to art galleries, shops, restaurants, hiking trails, and tours. Ride the free trolley to shops and galleries. Map & Directions

Luxurious Rooms

Stay in a well-appointed room in Casita Sedona with your own fireplace, king-sized bed, balcony overlooking the red rocks, private bath with shower, and whirlpool tub for two. Choose from the Canyon, Javalina, Manzanite, Thunder Mountain, and Schnebly rooms - each is unique and sure to be a high point of your vacation experience.

Hearty Breakfast

Each day starts with a hearty breakfast that includes your choice of belgian waffles, pumpkin french toast, or quiche. Fresh fruit, yogurt, juice, and coffee are always available.

About Your Hosts

Your hosts, Melanie and Gregorio Tortuga, are long-time Sedona residents who love to share their knowledge of the Sedona area with their guests. Melanie Tortuga is an author and artist. Gregorio has years of experience leading jeep tours and guiding hikes in and around Sedona.
